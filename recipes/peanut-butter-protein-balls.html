Peanut Butter Protein Balls

Meal Type: Snack

Time: 15 minutes

Equipment

  • 1x Large Bowl
  • 1x Baking Tray
  • 1x Baking Paper

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups Rolled Oats
  • 1 cup Milk Powder
  • 375 grams Peanut Butter
  • 1/2 cup Maple or Golden Syrup

Method

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the Milk Powder, Rolled Oats, Maple Syrup, and Peanut Butter and mix well
  2. Add a little milk or water to improve the consistency
  3. Divide the protein batter into 12 pieces at xx grams, roll into balls and place on the lined pan
  4. Refrigerate until firm and consume within 7 days
