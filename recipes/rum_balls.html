Spiced Rum Balls — Field Test v1

Time: 15 minutes

Equipment

  • 1x Small Pot
  • 1x Blender

Ingredients

  • Ingredients
  • 60mL Spiced Rum (alcoholic or non-alc)
  • 250g Arrowroot biscuits
  • 395g sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 cup desiccated coconut
  • ¼ cup cocoa powder
  • Additional coconut to decorate

Method

    1. Crush 250 grams of Arrowroot biscuits in a blender and transfer to bowl

    2. Add 395 grams of sweetened condensed milk

    3. Pour in 1 cup of desiccated coconut

    4. Strain in ¼ cup of cocoa powder 

    5. Measure and pour 60ml Bundaberg Rum flavour

    6. Mix well until all ingredients are combined

    7. Roll mixture into balls and cover in additional coconut

    8. Refrigerate and allow to harden
