Fried Rice v1.0

Meal Type: Dinner

Time: 30 minutes

Equipment

  • 1x Small Pot
  • 1x Frypan
  • 1x Tablespoon

Ingredients

  • 125 ml Rice
  • 250 ml Water
  • 1 Egg
  • 60 ml Peas
  • 60 ml Corn
  • 60 ml Vegetable Oil
  • 60 ml Sesame Oil
  • 60 ml Soy Sauce

Method

  1. Add Rice and Water to Pot and bring to boil (about 3 mins)
  2. Reduce to minimum temperature for 12 mins
  3. Rest for 5 minutes
  5. Heat Vegetable Oil in Fry Pan
  6. Add 1 Egg and lightly scramble
  7. Add Sesame Oil, Soy Sauce, Peas and Corn and stir in
  8. Add cooked Rice and fry for 15 mins stirring every minute adding soy for taste
