Creamy One-Pot Pasta — Field Test v2

Meal Type: Lunch Dinner

Time: 15 minutes

Equipment

  • 1x Small Pot
  • 1x Mug
  • 1x Tablespoon

Ingredients

  • Pasta: ~100 g
  • Water: 350 ml
  • Butter: ~10–15 g
  • Milk powder: 5 level tablespoons (≈25 g)
  • Cheese: 1 handful grated (≈35 g)
  • Salt: to taste

Method: 1x Mug

  • Add 5 level tablespoons milk powder (≈25 g) to mug
  • Add a little water and mix to a smooth paste
  • Top up with water to make 1 mug (~250 ml)
  • Stir until smooth (no lumps)

Method: 1x Pot

  • Add 350 ml water and pasta directly to the pot
  • Bring to the boil and cook until pasta is just done and most water is absorbed
  • Turn heat down low
  • Add butter and stir
  • Add milk mixture
  • Stir until it thickens and becomes creamy
  • Add cheese
  • Stir until smooth
  • Taste and add salt if needed
