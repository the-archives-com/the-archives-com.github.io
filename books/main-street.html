Main Street

Author: Sinclair Lewis

★★★★☆
Status : Read 2025
Year : 1920
Genre : Classic

Notes

I read this in mid 2025. It was on of the first books I started reading from the classics collections.

This one was interesting. It was about a girl from the city moving to a small town in America.

The most memorable moment was another girl's perspective, having come from a further town from the city again, thinking the small town was marvellous due to her familiar conditions.

It’s slow but interesting. Made me consider how life would have been in the 1920's, in a small town trying to improve it.

Reminded me off my Nanna’s town Numerkah in Northern Victoria. Interesting showing everyones point of views on different matters and their own self interest.

I would read it again in a few years.
