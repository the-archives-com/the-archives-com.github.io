Three Men in a Boat

Author: Jerome K. Jerome

★★★★☆

Notes

I read this last year in early 2025. I don’t remember the exact place, but I remember the feeling of it — it was easy to carry, easy to return to, and easy to live in for a while.

This one really stuck with me. Just a bunch of guys in olden times, on a boat, having fun and getting into mischief.

It’s light, human, and quietly funny. I really enjoyed it, and it left me with that rare feeling of having spent time somewhere pleasant.

I would happily read it again this year.
