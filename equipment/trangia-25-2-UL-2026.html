Trangia 25-2 UL Set

Manufacturer: Trangia

★★★★★

Notes

The versatility of the Trangia absolutely blows me away. It has 2 saucepans, a kettle and a frypan so can make a range of meals. The 1 Litre kettle is great for water purification or making a pot of tea. I like the patina effect on the cookware as it's used giving it an authentic character. I look forward to using this for the next 50 years and the stories that it will tell via it's ever changing finish. Of all of my camping equipment, this stands out as a favourite for portability, practicality and equisite design.
